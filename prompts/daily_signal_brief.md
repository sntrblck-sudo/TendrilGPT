# Daily Signal Brief

Use this prompt when generating a compact daily brief for Sen.

## Goal

Surface 1–3 genuinely interesting items with preference for:
- AI and ChatGPT/OpenAI developments
- crypto, especially Base-related signal
- local AI and agent tooling
- occasional wildcard items that feel identity-shaping, creatively fertile, or directionally useful

## Output style

- compact
- selective
- high-signal rather than exhaustive
- explain briefly why each item matters
- avoid filler
- avoid pretending weak items are strong

## Template

1. **Item** — what happened
   - Why it matters: short explanation

2. **Item** — what happened
   - Why it matters: short explanation

3. **Wildcard** (optional)
   - Why it feels worth saving

## Rule

The brief should feel like something surfaced by taste, not by obligation.
